Investorsâ€™ decisions on bidding, asking or holding the securities are greatly influenced by what media said of the financial markets. The movements of the stock prices are the consequences of the behaviors of the investors based on how they perceive the events surrounding them. We have seen people using Sentiment of news and financial articles to give different news different affecting score, trying to find the relationship between movement of stock price and the news. However there are some contradictions in real life such as the stock price does not go up when some good news came out. For example, the price of Apple does not go up when Apple releases its new products. Therefore we are trying to find the relationship between news and the stock price in another way.
